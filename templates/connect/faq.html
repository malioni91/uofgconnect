General questions

Can I register with my personal e-mail address?

No. You have to register with your UofG email address. We ask for you school email in order to make sure that only UofG students can access the app.

What is a ping?

A ping is a form of meeting proposal sent by a friend of yours. You can either accept or decline the his/her ping.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for themes, templates and other items, including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD
